# philosophic-filter (V3.0)

## Trigger
`/philosophic-filter --file <filename>`

## Use

Use this skill to transform ancient theological constraints into modern urban breakthroughs. V3.0 introduces **The Logic Auditor**, a high-fidelity gate that ensures structural adherence to the **Nietzschean Arc** and prevents rhetorical drift or academic jargon.

**The mission follows the V3.0 Logic Bridge:**

1.  **Brokerage Audit**: Reads Maimonides source and identifies the **Ancient Node**.
2.  **Urban Tension**: Identifies a specific modern disconnect (9-to-5 burnout, digital noise).
3.  **Bridge Contract**: Formulates the **Hypothesis** connecting Ancient constraint to Urban breakthrough.
4.  **Synthesis**: Generates the PT-BR sermon following the 4-phase Arc:
    *   **Pressure**: The weight of the constraint/tension.
    *   **Breakaway**: Saying "No" to the reactive noise.
    *   **Wilderness**: The difficult transition phase.
    *   **Renewal**: The new value or habit.
5.  **Logic Audit**: Mandatory pass through `scripts/logic_auditor.py` (Purges jargon + Verifies Arc).
6.  **Persist**: Registers the mission in `openclaw_intel.db` as a **Wisdom Vector**.

## Guiding Principles

**1. Warm Rigor.**
The tone must be Warm Rabbi (PT-BR), but the logic must be ruthless. No "Vibe-Washing."

**2. No Jargon.**
"Maimonides," "Nietzsche," and academic labels are strictly prohibited in the output. Translate the philosophy into life.

**3. Shared Wisdom.**
Every sermon contributes to the **Global Intelligence Store**, enabling cross-skill insights for the Tutor and Researcher.
