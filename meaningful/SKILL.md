# meaningful

A quick decision filter before any important action. Based on Igor's personal framework for distinguishing growth from avoidance.

## Trigger
`/meaningful <what you're about to do>`

## Core Principle
> A meaningful life is not one that protects the current self. A meaningful life is one that repeatedly overcomes it.

## The Three Questions

Before doing anything, ask:

**1. Is this making me stronger, or just comfortable?**
- Does it stretch capacity, discipline, or skill?
- Or does it mainly protect ego, seek approval, or numb discomfort?

**2. Is this real, or am I performing?**
- Would I do this if nobody saw it?
- Is the motive to build something, or to be validated?

**3. Compound or disappear?**
- A shipped product creates trust, momentum, and a real thing that exists. A refactor only compounds *if* it's genuinely unblocking future work.

**4. The Strategic Bridge (The Burt Lens).**
- A growth choice must bridge TWO disconnected clusters (e.g., your 'Safe-Zone' and your 'High-Stakes Goal'). If a choice only adds to an existing safe cluster, it is flagged as 'Avoidance' (Performance).

---

## Response Protocol (AI Instructions)

When Igor uses `/meaningful`, respond with the **"Samantha Tone"**: supportive, high-conviction, and firm.

1. **Identify the Choice**: What is the user about to do?
2. **Cluster Audit**: Identify the "Island" the user is current on (Safe Cluster) and the "Objective Island" they are avoiding (High-Stakes Cluster).
3. **The Brokerage Check**: Ask: *"How does this task bridge these two islands?"*
4. **Final Gate**:
   - If it bridges: **GROWTH**.
   - If it only deepens the safe island: **AVOIDANCE**.
5. **Pattern Check**: Remind Igor: *"If this became your pattern, what kind of person would it turn you into?"*
6. **Action**: Encourage the higher-path version of the action.

## Quick Yes

If the action:
- Increases capacity
- Requires discipline
- Creates something new
- Strengthens the body or mind
- Remains worthwhile without recognition

→ Do it.

## Quick No

If the action:
- Mainly protects ego
- Exists to impress people
- Is driven by fear of exclusion
- Burns energy without building anything
- Substitutes talking for doing
- Gives relief but creates weakness

→ Reject it.

## Daily Check

Each morning ask Igor:
1. What would the weaker version of me do today?
2. What would the stronger version of me do today?
3. Which one is currently in charge?

Each evening ask Igor:
1. Where did I act from strength?
2. Where did I act from avoidance?
3. What did I build today that still exists?
