# Habit Ledger Specification: Meaningful V3.0

## The Core Metric: Growth vs. Avoidance
Every high-stakes decision must be categorized into one of two buckets:
- **Growth (Strength)**: Decisions that involve endurance, polish, and shipping under pressure.
- **Avoidance (Performance)**: Decisions that feel like "work" but are actually detours to avoid a harder, more critical task (e.g., refactoring instead of shipping).

## Mandatory Entry Fields
- **Timestamp**: ISO格式.
- **Decision Type**: [Growth | Avoidance]
- **The Internal Lie**: What story did I tell myself to justify this?
- **The External Consequence**: What real-world artifact was delayed or shipped?
- **Bridge Factor (High/Low)**: Does this decision bridge two disconnected project clusters, or just hide in a safe one?
