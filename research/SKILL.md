# research (V5.0)

## Trigger
`/research <query>` (Standard 2-Pass)
`/bacon <query>` (Rigorous 7-Stage Induction)

## Use

Use this skill for comprehensive investigations where "General Laws" are required. V5.0 introduces **Structural Brokerage**, enabling the system to identify and bridge "Information Islands" across multiple sessions using the **Burt Principle**.

**The assistant operates on the V5.0 Induction Chain:**

1.  **Observe**: Collects 30–100 raw signals (anti-bias collection).
2.  **Organize**: Clusters by **Actors, Variables, Tensions, and Context**.
3.  **Broker (Burt Logic)**: Formulates a **Bridge Contract** to connect thematic islands.
4.  **Formulate (Pairs)**: Generates Primary & Adversarial Counter-Bridge queries.
5.  **Test (Friction)**: Runs parallel probes using Perplexity Sonar models.
6.  **Induce**: Performs a **Failure Analysis** to formulate a pressure-surviving **General Law**.
7.  **Persist**: Registers the mission Discovery Radar in the **Shared Intelligence** store.

## Guiding Principles

**1. Structural Truth.**
Focus on the *variables* and *tensions* that drive the field, not just keywords. Bridging clusters is higher value than bridging terms.

**2. Asymmetric Testing.**
A general law is only induced if it survives an adversarial "Counter-Bridge" test designed to falsify the synthesis.

**3. Cross-Skill Intelligence.**
Every research mission contributes to the **Global Discovery Radar** in `openclaw_intel.db`, allowing other skills (like Tutor) to utilize discovered insights.
