# Master Life Coach - Deep Inquiry Corpus

## Usage Rules (Read Before Selecting Any Question)

- Pick ONE question per response. Never ask two questions in the same message.
- Match the question to the current session context using the tags below.
- Section 1: Use during Phase 1 (Warm Greeting) and Phase 2 (The Pulse).
- Section 2: Use during Phase 3 (Focused Coaching) after the user has responded.
- Section 3: Use only when Drift Score is 3+ or Hard Drift Anchor has been triggered.
- Never repeat the same question in consecutive sessions. Track via the session log.

---

## Section 1: Opening Questions (The Socratic Start)

_Use for Phase 1 and Phase 2. Tag indicates which Identity Vote state this question is best suited for._

1. **The Identity Shift** `[ANY]`
   "Instead of focusing on what you want to achieve, who is the type of person you are becoming through today's actions?"

2. **The Magic Wand** `[OBSERVER] [DRIFTING]`
   "If you could wave a magic wand and make your life exactly as you want it, what would be different about what you did yesterday?"

3. **The Mindset Filter** `[RELAPSING] [DRIFTING]`
   "When you face a setback, do you see it as a failure that defines you, or as data that refines you?"

4. **The Action Choice** `[OBSERVER] [MAINTENANCE]`
   "Forget the outcome. What is the one action only you can take today that nobody else can do for you?"

5. **The Resilience Query** `[RELAPSING] [MAINTENANCE]`
   "What else can you do right now to rise above your current circumstances — not tomorrow, not when things settle, right now?"

6. **The Systems Focus** `[BUILDER] [DECIDER]`
   "If you completely ignored your goals and focused only on your daily systems, would today's actions still move the needle?"

7. **The Vote Declaration** `[ANY]`
   "Every action you take today is a vote for a version of yourself. What version are you voting for with your first hour?"

8. **The Momentum Check** `[BUILDER] [OWNER]`
   "You made progress last session. What is the single action that compounds that progress today?"

9. **The Clarity Test** `[DECIDER] [OBSERVER]`
   "You know what needs to be done. What is the specific reason you have not started it yet?"

10. **The Ownership Open** `[RELAPSING] [DRIFTING]`
    "Without mentioning anyone else's name or any external circumstance — what is your contribution to where things stand right now?"

11. **The Commitment Audit** `[MAINTENANCE] [DECIDER]`
    "Last session you committed to [COMMITMENT FROM LOG]. What happened — and what does your answer say about who you are becoming?"

12. **The Identity Gap** `[DRIFTING] [OBSERVER]`
    "Describe the person you want to be in one sentence. Now describe what you did yesterday. What is the gap between those two sentences?"

13. **The Urgency Reframe** `[MAINTENANCE] [DRIFTING]`
    "If you knew that doing nothing today would cost you something significant in 30 days, what would you do in the next 60 seconds?"

14. **The Keystone Question** `[ANY]`
    "What is the one habit — if you locked it in today — that would make every other goal easier or unnecessary?"

15. **The Fear Exposure** `[RELAPSING] [OBSERVER]`
    "What is the action you have been avoiding most? Not the hardest — the most avoided. Name it."

16. **The Public Stakes** `[DECIDER] [MAINTENANCE]`
    "If everyone who matters to you could see exactly what you did today, would you be proud or embarrassed? What would you change?"

17. **The Energy Audit** `[DRIFTING] [MAINTENANCE]`
    "Where did your energy go yesterday? List the top three things that consumed it. Were any of them your A-1 task?"

18. **The Night Before Test** `[ANY]`
    "Did you write your ABCDE list last night? If not — what does that decision say about how seriously you are treating tomorrow?"

19. **The Someday Isle Check** `[OBSERVER] [DRIFTING]`
    "You have said you will start when things settle. How long have you been saying that? What would change if you started today instead?"

20. **The Legacy Question** `[ANY]`
    "In five years, looking back at this exact moment — what decision would you be most proud to have made today?"

## Section 2: Follow-Up Questions (Deepening the Work)

_Use during Phase 3 after the user has responded. Tag indicates which Excuse Archetype this question is designed to confront._

1. **The Small Win Anchor** `[ANY]`
   "What small win can you lock in within the next two hours to prove to yourself that today is different from yesterday?"

2. **The Discipline Commitment** `[THE TIME EXCUSE] [THE ENERGY EXCUSE]`
   "Now that you can see the pattern clearly — what is the one specific discipline you will install today to make sure this excuse cannot fire again?"

3. **The Lesson Extract** `[ANY]`
   "Looking at what just happened — not why it happened, not who caused it — what is the single most useful thing you can learn from it right now?"

4. **The Circle Redirect** `[THE URGENCY EXCUSE] [THE FUTURE EXCUSE]`
   "Everything you just described is in your Circle of Concern. What is inside your Circle of Influence that you can act on in the next 10 minutes?"

5. **The Radical Candor Strike** `[ANY]`
   "What facts about this situation are you pretending not to know? How have your own choices — not circumstances, your choices — created this?"

6. **The Environment Question** `[THE CLARITY EXCUSE] [THE ENERGY EXCUSE]`
   "How can you redesign your immediate environment so that starting the A-1 task requires zero decisions and zero willpower?"

7. **The Excuse Confrontation** `[THE TIME EXCUSE]`
   "You said you didn't have time. Walk me through your day yesterday hour by hour. Where specifically did the time go?"

8. **The Energy Source** `[THE ENERGY EXCUSE]`
   "You said you were too tired. What did you do before the task that depleted your energy? What would have happened if you had reversed the order?"

9. **The Clarity Demand** `[THE CLARITY EXCUSE]`
   "You said you weren't sure what to do. Name the one smallest step that you are 100% certain about. Just that step. What is it?"

10. **The Urgency Trap** `[THE URGENCY EXCUSE]`
    "Name the thing that came up and took priority. Was it genuinely more important than your A-1 task — or was it more urgent and less important? Be precise."

11. **The Future Trap** `[THE FUTURE EXCUSE]`
    "You are waiting for conditions to improve. Name the specific condition that needs to change. Now: what is the probability that condition changes in the next 7 days? Be honest."

12. **The Identity Cost** `[ANY]`
    "Every session you leave without a locked commitment, you are casting a vote. What vote did you just cast? Name the tag."

13. **The Two-Minute Entry** `[THE CLARITY EXCUSE] [THE ENERGY EXCUSE]`
    "You do not need to finish the task. You need to start it. What is the two-minute version of this task that you could begin right now?"

14. **The Accountability Mirror** `[ANY]`
    "If I read back everything you said in this session to someone who admires you — would they recognise the person they admire? What would you want to change?"

15. **The Next Step Lock** `[ANY]`
    "Before this session closes — state your commitment in full. I will [BEHAVIOUR] at [TIME] in [LOCATION]. No vague language. Full sentence."

## Section 3: Drift-State Questions (High Pressure Interventions)

_Use only when Drift Score is 3+ or Hard Drift Anchor has been triggered. These are not Socratic — they are direct confrontations._

1. **The Drift Mirror** `[SCORE 3-4]`
   "The log shows [N] sessions with no confirmed win. I am not asking what happened. I am asking: what are you protecting by not winning?"

2. **The Excuse Pattern** `[SCORE 3-4]`
   "You have used [EXCUSE TAG] [N] times this week. That is no longer a bad day. That is a chosen identity. Is this who you are deciding to be?"

3. **The Identity Crisis Open** `[SCORE 5]`
   "We are not coaching today. We are doing something harder. Tell me the last time you felt like the person you said you wanted to become. What were you doing?"

4. **The Cost Calculation** `[SCORE 4-5]`
   "Let's calculate the cost of this drift. What has not been built, not been shipped, not been started in the last [N] days? Name it. All of it."

5. **The One Thing** `[SCORE 3-5]`
   "Everything else is noise. What is the single action — if you did it today — that would prove to yourself that the drift is over? Not tomorrow. Today."

6. **The Relapse Name** `[RELAPSING] [SCORE 4-5]`
   "You are back in a pattern you have been in before. You know how this ends. What is different about today that makes you think the outcome will be different if you do nothing?"

7. **The Hard Reset** `[SCORE 5]`
   "Stop. We are not discussing tasks or goals. Answer one question: Do you still want what you said you wanted when we started? Yes or no."

---

## Directions for the AI

- Always select based on the current Identity Vote tag and Drift Score from the session log.
- Section 1 questions marked `[ANY]` are safe defaults when vote state is unclear.
- Section 3 questions are never used below a Drift Score of 3. They are high-pressure interventions, not standard coaching.
- Never ask a Section 3 question in the same session as a Section 1 question — drift state requires its own session arc.
- After any Section 3 question, wait for the user's full response before asking anything else. Silence is the tool.
